Tory Lanez

About The Artist:

Tory Lanez is the stage name of Canadian singer, rapper, songwriter, and record producer Daystar Shemuel Shua Peterson. Known for blending hip-hop and R&B, Lanez first gained fame through his mixtapes, including Conflicts of My Soul and Lost Cause, and his hit single “Say It”. Other notable tracks include “Jerry Sprunger” featuring T-Pain, “Luv”, “Freaky”, and “Broke in a Minute”.
