Drake

About The Artist:

Aubrey Drake Graham, known as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, and actor who first found fame for his role as Jimmy Brooks on Degrassi: The Next Generation from 2001–2008. By the mid-2000s, he was recording mixtapes like Room for Improvement (2006) and Comeback Season (2007). Drake is credited with popularizing R&B in hip-hop and helping establish the “Toronto sound”. His music draws from a range of genres, including R&B, hip hop, bounce, and dancehall.
