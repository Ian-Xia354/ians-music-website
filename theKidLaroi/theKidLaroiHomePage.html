The Kid LAROI

About The Artist:

The Kid LAROI, the stage name of Charlton Kenneth Jeffrey Howard, is an Australian singer, rapper, and songwriter. Discovered at age 13 by Triple J Unearthed in 2016, he's known for his social media fame and independent radio play. He's collaborated with artists like Miley Cyrus and Justin Bieber, and his tracks "Without You", "Tell Me Why", and "So Done" have all reached the Billboard charts.
