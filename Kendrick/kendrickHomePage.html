Kendrick Lamar

About The Artist:

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer who has been called one of the greatest rappers of all time. Known for his social commentary and innovative wordplay, Lamar's music often references police brutality, systemic racism, and other political topics. In 2018, he became the first non-classical or jazz musician to win a Pulitzer Prize for Music for his album DAMN.
