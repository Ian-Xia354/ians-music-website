Daniel Caesar

About The Artist:

Daniel Caesar is the stage name of Canadian singer and songwriter Ashton Dumar Norwill Simmonds. Known for his calm, gospel-inspired sound, Caesar has won awards and critical acclaim for his music. In 2014, he impressed critics with his self-released EP Praise Break. His 2018 album Freudian won a Juno Award, and in 2019 he won a Grammy for Best R&B Performance for "Best Part".
